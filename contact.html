Immunization Clinic

Immunization Clinic is operated with the help of the Consultant in Public Health and Paediatrician. The clinic is held on the 3rd Wednesday of every month.



Organized Clinics

In recent times, as part of its initiative to promote preventive health care and early diagnosis, B C Roy Technology Hospital has organized clinics on:

  • Bone Mineral Density (BMD) Screening
  • Peripheral Neuropathy Screening
  • A Diabetic Screening Clinic has started from 8th March, 2014.


Emergency Unit

The Emergency Unit at B C Roy Technology Hospital is located on the ground floor of the hospital, close to the main entrance and the portico of the hospital, where the ambulance can bring a patient. The Unit provides emergency services 24x7. The Medical Officer on-duty attends the patient brought to the emergency, and is supported by Nursing and Paramedical Staff.

The unit is equipped with emergency life saving drugs, and several modern monitoring equipments including:

  • Multi Parameter Patient Monitor
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Automated External defibrillator (AED)

    • It also is supported by 24 Hours On-Call Pathology and X-Ray Lab Facility. 2 additional beds are available for patients under observation. Specialist Services are available to a limited extent.



    Inbuilt Pharmacy

    B C Roy Technology Hospital has a pharmacy within the Hospital Premises which is open 24x7 to provide the medicines at all hours to the campus community. It is outsourced to Frank Ross Pharmacy.

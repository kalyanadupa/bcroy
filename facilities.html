Lab Facilities

Pathology Lab

B C Roy Technology Hospital Pathology Laboratory is a reasonably well-equipped lab. It provides the routine Blood, Urine and Stool Examination services. In addition, the tests that the lab is equipped to conduct are:


  • Routine blood tests: TLC, DC, Hb, Platelets Count, PCV, ESR, Complete blood count (CBC), Mantoux Test
  • Blood Biochemistry: Blood Sugar, Urea, Creatinine, Uric Acid, Lipid profile
  • Liver Function Tests (LFT): Bilirubin, SGPT, SGOT, Alkaline phosphate, Total protein, Albumin, Globulin.
  • Blood electrolytes: Sodium, Potassium, Chloride

    • Malaria, Dengue, Hepatitis B, RTT/ WIDAL, HIV1 and 2, Hepatitis B (HBsAg), Anti- HCV, VDRL, RA Factor, ASO TITRE, CRP Tests etc. also can be done at the lab.


    Culture and Sensitivity Tests for Urine is available for indoor patients. Urine for Pregnancy test is also done at the lab.


    The equipments in the Pathology Lab include Cell Counter Machine, Semi-automatic Analyzer, Electrolyte Analyzer, Full Automatic Biochemistry Analyzer, Autoclave, Laminar Flow, Incubator.


    The attainment of quality service in a pathology lab depends on internal and external quality control. At B C Roy Technology Hospital, for quality assurance of the lab reports, the pathology lab has an internal control system. In addition, for external quality control, from January 2012 it is enrolled with the External Quality Assessment Scheme (EQAS), a 12 month program run by Christian Medical College, Vellore, under the banner of Association of Clinical Biochemists of India (ACBI). So far, the Pathology Lab at B C Roy Technology Hospital has been awarded two citations already for consistent quality maintenance in 2012 and 2013.



    Pathology sample Collection:

    The pathology sample collection is done at B C Roy Technology Hospital regularly from 8.00 AM to 10.00AM on all working days from Monday to Friday, excluding Institute Holidays. The collection is conducted and assisted by trained pathology lab staff.


    In addition, the lab provides on-call emergency services during the weekend.


    Pathology Report Collection:

    The Pathology reports may be collected Monday to Friday from 9.00 AM to 10.00 AM and 5.30 PM to 6.30 PM . The reports are certified by competent pathologists engaged by the hospital.

    Culture & Sensitivity Test of Urine & throat swabs are available for indoor cases to limited extent.

    The Radiology Unit at B C Roy Technology Hospital provides Digital X-Ray Facility to the campus community.

    Monday to Saturday- 9 AM to 12 Noon and 4 PM to 7 PM

    also, provides on-call service in an emergency



    Ultrasonography (USG):

    Diagnostic Sonography or Ultrasonography (USG) is an ultrasound-based diagnostic imaging technique used for visualizing internal body structures for better diagnosis. At B C Roy Technology Hospital, a visiting consultant in Radiology comes two days in a week to provide this service.


    The days and Timings are notified in the on-line notice board of B C Roy Technology Hospital, available through the Internal Notice Board of IIT Kharagpur.

    Electrocardiogram (ECG) facility is available in the Emergency unit of the B C Roy Technology Hospital.
